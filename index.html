Description

Like a warm hug for your eyes and mind, this self warming eye mask starts warming up the moment you open it. Lie back, get ready to relax and unfold the mask. When the mask meets the air it begins to warm up, staying at a comforting temperature and releasing the scent of Lavender, so you can drift into a daydream or a deep night’s sleep. A beautiful gift of self-care, the scent of Lavender can help relax, de-stress and assist with sleep.

Lavender Scent:
The scents are purposefully subtle so as not to overwhelm your sense while you relax. Lavender can help you relax, de-stress and assist with sleep. The scents are activated as the mask warms, so when the eye mask has heated, you can pull it down over your nose and take a few deep breaths in!

About the Lula Eye Masks in Lavender:
* There are 5 eye masks in each box
* The Lavender scent is the strongest of our scented eye masks.
* The eye mask is lightweight and very comfortable
* The warming ingredients are naturally occurring (iron powder, vermiculite, activated carbon, salt and water).
* Each eye mask warms once and can then be reused as a regular eye mask.
* The eye masks are made with non-woven fabric.

How to Use

Step 1: Open an individual pouch

Step 2: Unfold the mask and feel the mask gently warm up. When the mask’s iron particles meet oxygen in the air it begins to gradually warm up and stays at a comfortable temperature for a minimum of 20 minutes.

Step 3: Lie back, place the loops over your ears and mask on your eyes. Time to relax.

FAQ

How does the Lula Eyemask work?

The masks contain iron particles and when they come into contact with oxygen the iron slowly begins to warm, it’s a natural oxidation reaction. There are other natural ingredients that help this oxidising reaction to happen and to heat evenly; water, salt, charcoal and vermiculite.

The Lula Eye Masks are each sealed individually because once you open the pouch and air comes in contact it will start warming. It stays at a comforting temperature for a minimum of 20 relaxing minutes, so you can drift into a daydream or a deep night’s sleep.

What can it help with?

• Dry or tired Eyes
• Digital eye strain
• Busy minds
• Migraines & headaches
• Relaxation
• Meditation
• A good nights sleep
• Travelling

The warmth helps to relax the muscles around the eyes and also encourages circulation, helping to ease tension and strain. This can also help relieve headaches and migraines. As well as the Lula Eye Mask blocking light, it’s warmth feels soothing and calming, helping lull you to sleep. Like a warm compress the warmth creates a fine moist steam, comforting the skin around the eyes and giving relief to dry eyes.

How long will the Lula Eyemask stay warm?

The eye mask warms quickly reaching a comfortable temperature that will last for a minimum of 20 minutes and gently fades away by the time you’ve drifted off.

Can I reheat the eyemask?

No. Do not reheat the mask in any way. The Lula Eye Mask has been designed to warm up once to a safe, comfortable temperature for the eyes, it's a natural reaction when the iron particles in the mask meets the air and begins to heat. This reaction can only happen once, so although it can not be reheated you can keep the eye mask and continue to use as you would a normal sleeping eye mask to block out light.
